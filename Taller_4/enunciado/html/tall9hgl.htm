 Level (L) designates the center of the range of values mapped to grayscale. Window designates the extent of the range to either side of the level. Thus, the range mapped is (level-window/2) to (level+window/2). For a positive window value, regions less than (level-window/2) are clamped to black and regions greater than (level+window/2) are clamped to white. Regions at (level) are 50% gray. With a negative window value you get the inverse image.
